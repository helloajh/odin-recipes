Omelette

Pancakes probably can't get stacked as high as a building but then again I'm not a pancake expert

Ingredients

  • Pancake mix
  • Water
  • Patience

Steps

  1. Mix the mix with water until it's runny
  2. Heat up pain to medium heat and pour out the mix into a small circle
  3. Wait a few minutes, then flip and wait another few minutes
  4. Eat!
